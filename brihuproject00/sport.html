Sports

Over the years I've done a lot of sports

I've played tennis, swam, ran, competed in triathlons and now most recently, I row for my school. As you can see, most of the sports I do/did are pain sports.


Here's the breakdown

Rowing


  • Brutal sport, probably ties with running as being the physically most painful sport in my opinion
  • Correct me if I'm wrong, but one of the only team based, pain sports. I think out of everything I've done, rowing is my favorite for this reason.
  • Caloric intake - med to high

Running


  • Also a brutal sport, if you're legs are anything like mine you can expect shin splints and if you're really unluck a stress fracutre
  • Kinda team based but more of a solitary sport
  • Caloric intake - high

Swimming


  • Out of my experience, not the most painful, but that's probably becasue I did it for a long time. Definetely most taxing on the lungs
  • Probably more individual than running becasue you're literally staring at a black line. But relays are fun. Burn't out sophomore year of highschool cause I switched teams and they sucked
  • Caloric intake - med to high

Triathlons


  • The grand whammy, swimming, biking and running. I personally enjoy this a ton. Gives you lots of time to think about life
  • Ultimate individual sport
  • Caloric intake - uber high depending on volume

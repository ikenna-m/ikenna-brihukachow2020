My Story

I am an only child. Currently live in Ohio, but have been around to a lot of places. Grew up in Naperville, Illinois and did highschool in Sugar Land, TX. I know, it's a great name.

Childhood

I grew up in Naperville. Lots of my childhood is filled with memories of going to the park after school and playing with friends. Those were the good old days. One of my favorite games was playing wall ball against our school wall. I can't imagine how many tennis balls we threw on the roof. Woodchips was another favorite. Just thinking back is nostalgic.

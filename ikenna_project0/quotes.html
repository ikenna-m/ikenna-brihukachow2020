Here are some quotes that I like :)

כל העולם כולו גשר צר מאוד והעקר לא לפחד כלל is one said by the Rabi Nachman of Breslov. It means, "The whole world in its entirety is a very narrow bridge, and the main thing is to not have no fear at all." There's a really pretty song that is associated with it and I really like the imagery of this one!
